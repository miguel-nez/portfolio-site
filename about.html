About

My Story

I am a graduate in Intercultural Studies, German-Spanish focus. I previously did three years of Architecture and found that, in order to have a bigger impact on people’s lives, I needed to deepen my understanding of their vital experiences.

I am committed to creating a fairer, care-taking society. I want to understand privilege so I can contribute to reduce inequality: I believe in the potential of digital products as a tool to achieve this, so that’s why I chose to explore the path of UX Design.
